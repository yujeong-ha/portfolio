Dazzley, (2024)

Overview

A gamified approach to make mornings more exciting for kids

Roles

UX/UI Design Branding Gamification Research

Tools

Figma Photoshop Illustrator Visual Studio Code

Project duration

Sep 2024 – Nov 2024

Problem

Children often struggle to prepare themselves independently in the mornings for school.

Research question

How can I utilisecharacter designs & interactive technologies to gamify the going to the school experience and make it more exciting for kids?

Project Goal

To create an app that empowers children to independently prepare for their day through a game-like experiencein the morning, easing the busy routines for both children and parents.

Target audience

Our target audience encompasses both the children and parent, as children will interact with our app, while parents are supervising them using the app. Our primary target audience are busy working parents and children aged 5–9 years old, those who are attending the primary school.

Process / Outcomes

The app

Walk through – building up the narrative

to strengthen the bond between users and the app, I created a narrative that introduces the characters as fairy school graduates sent to help kids with their morning routines. This backstory makes the characters feel like companions, not just app features. I also implemented a daily streak system, where rewards increase the more days children use the app, creating excitement and anticipation.

Walk through – Gamification

I focused on gamifying task completion to make the experience more engaging. The app presents one task at a time with the time limit, with a photo confirmation step using character frames to add a fun, interactive element. Users earn coins every time they complete a task, which they can use to buy costumes for their characters, adding an extra layer of motivation.
